Closed system

Flash is owned and controlled by Adobe. It's a closed system. It is being replaced with HTML5 standard which is faster, smoother and works natively. Standards always win.

It's only the video

Video playback keeps Flash alive. HTML5 has video tag but it lacks streaming, embedding and camera support. Without those Flash would be dead by now. On other areas Flash communities are silent.

Steve Jobs

Apple drops the support for Flash. This is not a small signal. Programmers move away from risky technology. They want to be part of a vibrant ecosystem and secure their future. The future is in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
