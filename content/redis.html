Disks will kill you

Tape operations killed us in the past. Now it's disk IO. Forget them. RAM is the future. Apple Air does not have disks. Redis performs everything in memory. Why? It's so fast that it scares you.

Relational is hard(er)

Redis datatypes are humane. Lists, maps and sets are native for us programmers. Tables, columns, and rows are not not. We need to use use hacks or middleware to make them feel natural.
